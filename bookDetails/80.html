Overview

The last thing in life I ever expected was walking into a career caring for pets! A sudden change of gears led me to say goodbye to corporate life and hello to a crazy, new lifestyle ranging from surreal and tender moments to unexpected adventures. These behind the scenes stories share how I started and built a successful pet care business with comical and crazy misadventures along the way! What started as a casual part-time business quickly grew and flourished into a high demand 24/7/365 work-life and swiftly expanded from working solo to a team of 10. Little did I know how demanding this career was going to be, yet the most organically rewarding too. As a child, I had an instinct with animals. That developed as I got older and I noticed I was able to communicate and connect with our furry friends. The animals knew of our unique connection before I did, and the mutual gravitation was inevitable!
